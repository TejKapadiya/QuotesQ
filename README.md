# QuotesQ

Turn any screen into a source of daily inspiration.

QuotesQ is a simple motivational quote display designed for a second monitor, TV, or any fullscreen display. It automatically rotates through inspiring quotes while showing beautiful background images, creating a calm and motivating workspace.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌄 Beautiful fullscreen background images
- 💬 Inspirational quotes that change automatically
- 🎨 Dynamic fonts and styling
- 🌈 Dynamic accent colors
- 🕒 Live clock and date
- ⏳ Automatic quote rotation every minute
- ⏭️ Skip button to instantly view the next quote
- ⚡ Smooth transitions and preloaded backgrounds
- 📺 Perfect for second monitors and TVs
- 🖥️ Clean, distraction-free fullscreen interface

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Make sure your computer is connected to the internet.
2. Open your favorite web browser.
3. Visit:

   **https://tejkapadiya.github.io/QuotesQ/Index.html**

4. Press **F11** (or use your browser's **Full Screen** option).
5. Enjoy your personal motivational display.

---

## 💡 Best Experience

QuotesQ works best when displayed on:

- 🖥️ A second monitor
- 📺 A TV
- 🏢 Office lobby displays
- 📚 Study rooms
- 🏠 Home office setups
- ☕ Workstations

Simply leave it running in fullscreen mode and let the inspiration flow.
